Welcome to our community

WORDS AND PICTURES is Gateshead's celebration of the comic book community. It is an event for fans of all types of comics and aims to support creators and promote discussion about diversity and inclusivity within the comics industry.
By coming along you aren't just attending an event, you're joining the community.

Guests include:

  • Kelly Thompson
  • Kyle Higgins
  • Jen Bartel
  • Rachel Smythe
  • Gene-Luen-Yang
  • Gail Simone

Highlights include:

  • Mid-Con Party @ By the River Brew
  • Panels & talks
  • Portfolio Reviews
  • Signings
  • Cosplay
  • Exclusive anthology
